Frequently Asked Questions

What is MMHS?

MMHS or Micro Mobile Healthcare Solution is a patented technology that seeks to deliver healthcare insurance in micro payments by leveraging last mile mobile technologies like SMS, USSD and Airtime.

What is Mhino?

Mhino is "the Last Mile Mobile Health Platform for Activation, Registration, Collection and Ecosystem Management”...it is Digital Health Inclusion Solution based on the MMHS technology platform

Who is MHINO for?

Mhino is for everyone who previously could not access healthcare services due to high initial fees, by breaking down the Lump Sum into micro payments to ease the burden.

How can I get a Mhino Plan?

Mhino plans are delivered in partnership with health maintenance organisations and governments who have designed health insurance products for access.

Why should I use Mhino?

Mhino reduces the burden of out-of-pocket expenses in your access to healthcare delivery services. It provides more revenues for the Ecosystem to be able to deliver health services.

As a Government how can I partner with Mhino?

You can partner up with us to collect revenues in Micro payments for your health insurance programs instead of one-off bulk payments by breaking down the payment over a particular period and managing settlement with healthcare providers (HCPs) and the government./p>

As an HMO how can I partner with MHINO?

Mhino provides the HMO with the opportunity to breakdown their lump collection payments into micropayments over a particular period before healthcare services are delivered

What is the gestation period?

The gestation period is the timeframe within which you have to complete the minimum threshold payment to be able to access health insurance services

Is Mhino secure?

Yes, Mhino is secure by leveraging Industry leading platforms, NIN, end-to-end encryption, etc.

How can I pay for MHINO?

You can pay for Mhino by obtaining a NUBAN-linked Mhino Wallet that allows you to pay your premiums using USSD, POS, Bank Transfer, etc.
